There are 5 types of place which where categorized by tamil people based on characteristic of that landscape - including flora and fauna, inhabitants, deities and social organisation and based on where they live as follows.

  • Kurinji | Hilly/Mountain region
  • Mullai | Grasslands/Base of hills - livestock raising(shepherds)
  • Marutam | Crop Lands/Agricultural lands
  • Neithal| Ocean/Coastal area
  • Palai | Desert/Dry lands

0. The Praise Of Lord

Location : Kurinji - Hilly/Mountain region

Author : Paaratham Paadiya Perunthaevanaar

Your feet of a deity is as beautiful as flower of lotus
Endless shine of light is your body of coral
Your red colored dress is as of rosary pea plant
Your bright lance will pierce chest of any hills
Holding a cock flag with your pretty beautiful-ness
Creatures ever has joyous days cause you guard

Translated by ThamoNature

Lord Murugan

Lotus

Feet

Coral

Cock Flag

Coral Body

Red color dress

Rosary pea plant

Lance

Mountain Gap(cheravanji mali)


Explanation :

Lord Murugan’s feet is as beautiful as lotus flower, who does all the favour for his beloved followers.
Like Coral reef his body is shiny and beautiful and it’s a never fading light for those who seeks him.
He wears red colored dress which is similar to the plant Abrus Precatorius.
He holds lance which is so bright and so it can pierce the mountains named cheravanji mali.
His stands bold with his flag which has a beautiful cock symbol.
Each and every creature in this world ever has an peaceful and joyous days because Lord Murugan protects the world.

Scene : Heroine’s friend refusing the Gloriosa Superba flower and not allowing hero to see her heroine by saying we have a mountain of these flowers, we no need it... but hero heartfully want to hear her so he brought the flowers.

Author : Thippu Tholar

Battle fields were red with blood, none your
- enemies left behind cause you killed all.
The tip of arrow and elephant tusk were red
The field was a little mountain where lord Murugan
wearing whirling pride bracelets
In the mountain where Gloriosa Superba flower

Translated by ThamoNature

Red battle field

Blood bows

Tusk with blood

Flame Lily

pride bracelets


Explanation :

Hero is planning to reach heroine through heroine friend by giving the flower and saying give it to my love.
however she refused to accept the gift(glory lily) and saying these flowers were already there in our residence
(mountain and mountain related place – Kurinji),Therefore we no need it and somewhere the heroine
actually hiding herself from the sight of hero and let her friend to deal him.
Those were the days the flower was so precious amazing gift for us,
when you and her in love but now I don’t need the flower anymore,
we have a mountain of the flower and she is not letting him to meet the her
Lord Murugan is the ruler of the mountains, he who kills all enemies
and left battle field were red with blood where arrows and elephant tusks were with blood and there he wearing his pride whirling bracelets,
among the mountains where grows the Gloriosa Superba flower(Glory lily). That's the flower hero is giving to heroine friend here, the flower has that much pride as it grows in the hill areas where the lord murugan guards.

3. Heroine Explains Her Love For Hero

Location : Kurinji - Hilly/Mountain region

Scene : Hero is standing behind the fence while audibly heroine’s friend telling he'll not marry you so heroine explains her love for him.

Author : Thaevakulathar

Wider than the Earth, higher than the Sky,
His value immeasurable to the depth of the Ocean
My mountain man lives where bees collects surplus honey
From Strobilanthes Kunthiana(kurinji) flower with dark grey stem.

Translated by ThamoNature

Wider than the Earth

Higher than the Sky

Depth of the Ocean

Strobilanthes Kunthiana(kurinji)


Explanation :

He who seeks only sexual pleasure from you
He who comes only by the backdoor
Do you still believe in his love and his words
confronts that he will marry you ?.
Hero Hears all these said by heroine's friend while standing behind the fence.
However heroine replies that he is not that kind of man
He is man of mountains
My love for him is wider than the Earth
higher than the Sky and
his value immeasurable to the depth of the Ocean
he lives in a place where bees collects surplus honey
from Strobilanthes Kunthiana(kurinji) flower with dark grey stem.
A man from such a place might not cheat on me
My love for him is endless and he is the only man who came behind my fence
